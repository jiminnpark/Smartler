Instructions

Start-End of the Day

You have to specify the Start and End time of your day so that we can move forward!

Tasks

You have to specify the name of your Tasks that you want us to add in your schedule!

Fixed Tasks

Here you have to tell us some of your Fixed tasks (ex- Lunch, Gym, Dinner, Family time, Movie time, etc) to be included in Schedule!

Schedular Result

Finally, the algorithm calculates the personalised schedule based on your the Tasks you have specified as well as the Start and the End of the Day

Please remember..

The Freetime is added for the sake of your convenience so that your schedule dont overfit, It can also be used as expanded time for previous Task; it has been added considering that your previous Task might be pending!

The Algorithm may not work if:
  • You have specified invalid Start and End of the Day.
  • You have added invalid time for Fixed tasks.
  • You have repeated the Tasks.
